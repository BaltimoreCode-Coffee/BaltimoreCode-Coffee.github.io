Baltimore Code & Coffee Summer Workshop Series #2

Date and Time:

2024-09-07 10:00:00 EST

Location

Digital Harbor Foundation Tech Center

Description

**What We Do:** Code & Coffee is a place where people with passion for technology can come together, exchange ideas, & learn from one another. Our job is to facilitate that exchange. Code & Coffee Summer Workshops **Topic:** Communication Skills for Developers: Speak Up Outside of Stand Up! **Presenter:** Barry Wright "The more you progress in your career as a software developer, the more critical communication skills become. Advocating for your work, clarifying requirements, and collaborating across teams are all crucial expectations, and they all need you to speak up!" In this workshop, Barry will show us the core pieces of effective communication and give you plenty of opportunities to practice initiating discussion, reflecting back what you’re hearing, and making key connections between people. **SCHEDULE:** 10:00 AM: Begin 10:30 AM: Intro Circle 10:45 AM: Workshop Begins 1:00 PM: End **PARKING:** There is metered street parking. If all else fails, park at the West Street Garage. Residents of Baltimore near Charles St should consider the free Charm City Circulator bus. **HOW TO FIND US** Please use the main door at 1045 Light Street. If the door is closed, please ring the doorbell. FREE EVENT!

Baltimore Code & Coffee Demo Day

Date and Time:

2024-09-28 11:00:00 EST

Location

FastForward U, Johns Hopkins University

Description

Code & Coffee's Demo Day is a chance for members of the local tech community to show off the cool projects they’ve been working on. Come and enjoy our four participant’s ten minute demos with 5 minutes for questions. After the demos, each of the participants will have their own table for you to ask more questions about their work in depth. After 30 mins of this, we will have a vote for the winner. **SCHEDULE:** Networking - 11:00am-11:30am Demos - 11:30am-12:30pm Table Talk - 12:30pm-1:00pm Vote 1:00pm-1:10pm Networking - 1:10pm - 2:00pm **PARKING:** If you are driving to visit us, we suggest parking in the R House overflow lot that is catty corner from R House across W. 29th St. It’s shared with the Baltimore Police Department, and free to park there.
